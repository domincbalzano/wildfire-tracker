About my project

I created a real time wildfire tracking dashboard, it consolidates NASA satellite fire data and saved locations into one interactive map.

THE PROBLEM

Wildfires are one of the most destructive natural disasters in North America, particularly across the western United States. Despite the scale of this threat, most people have limited access to a single easy to use tool that shows where fires are actively burning.

TARGET AUDIENCE

General Public

Residents of fire prone states who need to make decisions about evacuation or outdoor activity safety.

Emergency agencies

Local and state agencies that coordinate fire response and public safety advisories.

Outdoor Enthusiasts

Hikers and campers who need to assess whether planned routes are currently safe.

DATA SOURCES

NASA FIRMS

Provides real time active fire hotspot data derived from satellite imagery, updated multiple times daily.

Supabase

Stores and retrieves saved locations so users can bookmark specific coordinates to monitor.
